Engineema, a cinema for engineers

Enginemaa is a project specially dedicated to engineering professionals and students who want to expand their knowledge horizons; thus, the website is filled with all kinds of engineering documentary movies (computer science, programming, networks, electronics, mechanics, etc.), so people that watch them can learn new things about our modern industry

Anytime and anywhere

Engineema has many theaters so you have the freedom to enjoy your favorite documentary movies in any of them with an enormours variety of showtimes!

Learn about engineering

From mechanics to even biology, in Engineema you can search and find a lot of scientific topics in movie format, thereby learning with an alternative and entertaining method!

Use any currency

You can pay your tickets with any currency through Engineema's special bank exchange machines!
